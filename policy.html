Company Policy

At TECG we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and inclusivity in all aspects of our recruitment and staffing operations. Our company policy outlines the principles and procedures that guide our interactions with clients, candidates, employees, and partners. We uphold a strict code of conduct that promotes ethical behaviour, respect for diversity, and zero tolerance for discrimination or harassment.

Our recruitment practices are transparent, merit-based, and compliant with applicable labor laws in both the United States and India. We ensure that all candidate data is handled securely and in accordance with global data protection regulations such as GDPR and CCPA. Our engagement with clients is governed by clear service agreements, confidentiality clauses, and performance benchmarks to ensure mutual success. Internally, we foster a supportive work environment with flexible work arrangements, fair leave policies, and regular performance reviews.

We also prioritize cybersecurity, ensuring that all digital tools and platforms used in our operations are secure and responsibly managed. This policy is reviewed periodically to reflect evolving legal standards, industry best practices, and the needs of our growing team and clientele.
